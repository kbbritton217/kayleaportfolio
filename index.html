About me

What's up with me?

As a life-long learner making the decision to pursue web development was not a difficult choice to make. I strive to use both my creative tendencies and my need for structure and consistency and Tech is the best marriage of those skills. I combine those abilities with the bevy of soft skills I've gained in the last 10 years, such as problem-solving and the ability to work individually or on a team, to produce quality and efficient projects.

Along with this, my years of experience in Higher Education and customer service have given me the ability to effectively communicate both verbally and written with any client from any background.
